about me

A professional Data Engineer with extensive experience working with data with a focus on GIS, as well as Editor data creation/editing tools and georeferencing tolerances.

Experience in various stages of the data pipeline, including queries, integrations, ODS, and real-time data marts.

Continuously looks at ways to add value to take the initiative in improving processes and identifying business opportunities.

An evangelist of geographic information systems, I believe that 90% of the data can be displayed on a map.

My functions at the moment:

- ### Dashboards Development of a complete application stack for dashboards, including database design and management, ETL processes (extract, transform and load), statistical analysis and data visualization.

- ### Database Applications I'm building custom database applications to facilitate multi-user role-based data management with full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) capabilities.

- ### Web Applications Development of web applications with a full range of possibilities. I manage every phase of development, including UI/UX conceptual design, full stack development, QA/QA, application hosting, deployment, and ongoing support and maintenance.

- ### WebGIS I am building my own cost effective web GIS applications using MS SQL, GISServer, ArcGIS Enterprise, Leaflet. I provide an ever-growing list of basic tools such as search and zoom, identification, filtering, printing and data export. Custom instruments can also be created. I provide ongoing support and maintenance for all my creations.

- ### Mobile Data Collection I help users make informed work decisions with highly accurate data collection considering XYZ accuracy, GNSS devices, and multi-user editing scenarios.

- ### Geospatial Analysis Geospatial analysis is used by many sectors, including commercial, government, academia, environmental, public health, and more. I use geospatial analysis to solve problems, improve productivity, and improve location awareness. Possible applications include:: Erosion modelling in agricultural or coastal areas. Examining the distribution of archaeological assemblages. Monitoring conservation areas. Defining food deserts and swamps in urban areas. Precision agriculture and farm management. Watershed and subwatershed delineation using 3D terrain analysis.

- ### Remote sensing I specialize in the processing, analysis and optimization of Earth observation data. I work with a wide range of sensors and platforms including Landsat, Sentinel, airborne sensors, LiDAR and BPLA and many more. Remote sensing allows: Conduct land cover and land use change analysis. Identify terrain changes over time. Analyze agricultural growth patterns and deficiencies. Quantify and map forest health. Identify and map features in imagery. Monitor municipal bylaw compliance.

- ### Spatial Databases Design, creation and management of spatial databases. I create fully managed cloud and on-premises enterprise databases that use trigger functions, third-party data wrappers, vector tiles, network analysis, materialized views, and spatial relationships: Full database management Basic and advanced layer editing Spatial analysis within the database Default symbology Constraints Data import/export functions User and group role management

- ### GIS I have been working with GIS, both commercial and open source, for over a decade. GIS software is a feature rich platform that allows you to create true ecosystems of geospatial data. I develop desktop, web and/or mobile solutions with many GIS applications such as: ArcGIS Enterprise ArcGIS Online ArcGIS Pro ArcGIS Developer ArcGIS Business Analyst QGIS Geoserver PostGIS GDAL GRASS SAGA GIS
